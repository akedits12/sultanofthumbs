Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our thumbnail services

How long does it take to receive my thumbnail?

Our standard delivery time is 24-48 hours, depending on your package. The Pro package includes 24-hour delivery, while our Premium package offers express 12-hour delivery. If you need it even faster, contact us for rush options.

What information do you need to create my thumbnail?

We'll need your video title, a brief description of the content, any specific elements or text you want included, your branding guidelines (if applicable), and any reference images you'd like us to use. The more details you provide, the better we can match your vision.

How many revisions can I request?

The Basic package includes 2 revisions, while both the Pro and Premium packages include unlimited revisions. We're committed to ensuring you're completely satisfied with the final result.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive your thumbnail in high-resolution JPG and PNG formats, optimized for YouTube (1280x720px). Pro and Premium packages also include editable source files (PSD or AI) so you can make future adjustments.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the design despite revision attempts, we'll issue a full refund. However, we're confident in our work and will do everything to ensure you're satisfied with the results.
